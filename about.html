Our Beginnings

The Story Behind the Fortress

Faith Fortress was founded in 2005 by a small group of believers who gathered in a living room with a singular vision: to build a community where every person could encounter the love of God, discover their purpose, and find a family.

What began as a modest prayer meeting has grown into a thriving church of over 2,500 members — a testimony not of human effort, but of divine faithfulness. Through seasons of growth, challenge, and breakthrough, our foundation has remained unmovable: Christ is the cornerstone.

Today, Faith Fortress stands as a beacon of hope in our city — a place where the broken find healing, the lost find direction, and the weary find rest.

🎯

Our Mission

To lead people into a transformative relationship with Jesus Christ, equip them for purposeful living, and send them out as agents of change in their communities and the world.

👁

Our Vision

To be a global community of fearless, compassionate, Spirit-empowered believers who demonstrate the Kingdom of God in every sphere of society — family, business, arts, and government.

📜

Our Values

Faith. Integrity. Excellence. Compassion. Unity. We hold these values not as ideals but as lived commitments in everything we do — in worship, in service, and in community.

What We Believe

Our Core Beliefs

01

The Bible

We believe the Holy Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God — our ultimate authority for faith and practice.

02

The Trinity

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — co-equal and co-eternal.

03

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone — His life, death, and resurrection provide redemption for all humanity.

04

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, through whom God empowers, guides, and transforms believers.

05

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ — called to worship, disciple, and demonstrate God's love to the world.

06

The Second Coming

We believe in the personal, imminent return of Jesus Christ and the resurrection of both the saved and the lost.
